Instructions/How To Win/How to Lose --- Building a house adds a new villager. Each villager adds an action point allowed per day. At the end of everyday, you lose food and water depending on how many villagers you have and your action points will reset. Once you build your 5th house and get your 5th villager, you win the game. Be tactical though, hitting 0 Food or Water means a villager will die every turn one is at 0. If you run out of villagers, you lose.
